To move left press "A" on keyboard or whatever you have assigned to "horizontal -" in Unity
To move right press "D" on keyboard or whatever you have assigned to "horizontal +" in Unity
To move right press "Spacebar" on keyboard or whatever you have assigned to "Jump" in Unity

Collect coins and finish the level as fast as possible to gain the most points possible.
Jump on top of foxes to kill them before they kill you.
